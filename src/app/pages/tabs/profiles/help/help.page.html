Sending and Receiving:

Money are received in two ways in VELUXPAY, using the Scan-to-go or Tag Name features. Navigate to RECEIVE on your dashboard Scan your QR code overs the senders camera or asked the user to copy your tag name Sent money is received immediately To send money Navigate to SEND on your dashboard To send money use the scanner button located at the top right corner of the screen and scan a QR code You can also click the add receiver button to type a user tag name and select the right one Enter the desired amount to be sent Proceed to input your PIN which was sent to your email on creation of your VELUXPAY account or the new one you created when prompted to.

Sub-Account:

Sub-accounts are convenient feature that allow you to create and manage multiple accounts within your main account. You can use sub-account to separate your income just to help know the total money coming from an angle. To create a sub-account: Navigate to your profile located at the bottom right of your screen Click on Account Locate sub accounts and click on it Click on “create new sub account” Your sub account tag name and password will be generated for you.
